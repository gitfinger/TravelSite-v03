About the Author

Tamsin Crimmens

Tamsin Crimmens is a freelance journalist who has written for British newspapers, including the Guardian and the Independent, as well as magazines ELLE, Stylist and others. Somewhat a digital nomad, she has lugged her laptop from London to Barcelona, and Northumberland to Nepal, to write about arts and culture, social trends and travel.
