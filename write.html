Write

Broader Horizons depends on experienced travellers with no ties to commercial interests. We are intending to build our stock of destination reviews cautiously and carefully. We value reliable judgment, intelligent insight and high quality writing.

If you feel you have these qualities, have just come back from somewhere off the well-worn track and want to tell like-minded people why they should go there — what they'll experience and what the barriers are to getting at it — then please contact us here.
